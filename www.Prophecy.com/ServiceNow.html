ServiceNow is a cloud-based workflow & automation system which automates enterprise service operations by creating a single system for all business processes within an organization. This system brings together strategy, design, transition and operation with ongoing management that can transform and modernize an enterprise.

ServiceNow IT Service Management (ITSM) applications are built on a single platform-as-a-service which offers a consistent and intuitive user experience through the entire IT Infrastructure Library (ITIL) service lifecycle and are used to support functions that include Business Process Management, Case Management, Change Management, Problem Management, Operations and Support.

PROPHECY is a leading ServiceNow partner that provides IT Service Management (ITSM) solutions to organizations worldwide. As a ServiceNow partner, PROPHECY leverages the powerful capabilities of the ServiceNow platform to help organizations streamline their IT operations and improve service delivery.

ServiceNow ITSM is a comprehensive suite of IT service management tools that enables organizations to manage their IT infrastructure and services more efficiently. The platform offers a wide range of functionalities, including incident management, problem management, change management, and asset management, all of which are critical for effective IT service delivery.
